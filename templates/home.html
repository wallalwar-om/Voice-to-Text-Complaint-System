How to Use SpeakUpp

🗂️ Register Your Complaint

Visit the homepage and click "Register Complaint." Select a complaint category: Water, Electricity, Roads, Garbage, or Other. Select the language and upload your audio file. Click "Submit Complaint."

📤 Upload Audio File

Formats supported: MP3, WAV, WEBM, OGG. Max size: 10 MB. Make sure the selected language matches the audio. Speak clearly to ensure accurate transcription.

🔐 For Admins

Click "Admin login" to login as admin. Superadmin has got powers such as add new admins and accessing all complaints.

💡 Notes

Only audio files are accepted. Speak clearly. Make sure your file matches the selected language. For support, visit the Help Center.
