How to Use SpeakUpp

🗂️ Register Your Complaint

Click "Register Complaint," choose a category, select a language, upload audio, and submit.

📤 Upload Audio File

Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, WEBM. Max size: 10MB. Speak clearly for better transcription.

🔐 For Admins

Superadmins can add admins and manage all complaints. Click "Admin Login" to sign in.

💡 Notes

Only audio files are allowed. Speak clearly. Ensure language matches audio. Need help? Visit Help Center.
