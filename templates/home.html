How to Use SpeakUpp

🎤 Record Your Complaint

Click on "Start Recording" to record your voice complaint directly in the browser. Ensure your microphone access is allowed.

📁 Upload Audio File

If you already have a recorded audio file, just upload it in the provided field. Supported formats include MP3, WAV, and WEBM.

📂 Categorize Your Complaint

Select the relevant category for your complaint (e.g., Water, Electricity, Garbage). This helps the admin sort and respond better.

📬 Submit and Relax

After submission, your complaint will be logged and visible in the admin dashboard. You'll get a confirmation on success.
